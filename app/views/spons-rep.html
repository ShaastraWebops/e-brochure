Shaastra

The annual technical festival

of Indian Insitute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India

Shaastra, the fiesta of Science, Technology and Entertainment, ever since its inception in the year 2000 has always striven to make a difference in the world we live in through significant creative and innovative advancements in every walk of life, acheived by the very people who come, witness and involve themselves with us throughout the days of shaastra. We provide a huge platform to everyone for simply acheiving perfection, for discovering their talents and designing their dreams by breaking all boundaries.

We are the SPIRIT OF ENGINEERING!




Shaastra's Vision

To be a world-class technical festival under its parent institution continuously striving to push the boundaries in the spheres of technology, engineering and innovation. Shaastra aims to provide a platform for continuous faculty-student-academic collaboration and interaction as well as promoting new frontiers of thought by the student community.

Shaastra's Mission

To create and sustain a community that encourages thought and learning in which students can acquire knowledge through interaction with fellow students as well as the industry.

To encourage students to explore new frontiers in technical as well as management process.

To create and sustain a culture that enables the industry to recognize and reard talent in engineering.

Quality Policy

To pursue excellence in all activities pertaining to Shaastra namely technical event creation and management, outreach activities, management, corporate relations and website management through the process of constant re-evaluation and self improvement.

Shaastra is the ISO 9001 :2008 certified student organized technical festival. A dedicated division will be responsible for implementation of the ISO 9001 standards at Shaastra. Enhancing and driving efficiency in the processes of inter- departmental collaboration and implementing exhaustive documenting policies are the approaches adopted to ensure quality and transparency in the system. Certification stands for the quality of perfection Shaastra has achieved with its present system and the attitude towards continual improvement which is made possible by the professionalism and overwhelming enthusiasm of the Shaastra organizing team.

Since it's inception, Shaastra's aim has been to explore activity outside the classroom and to spark creative, innovative and hand's-on approaches to a variety of problems from varying fields relating to technology. Over the years, the festival has evolved and now the grandeur and scale of events, is for all to behold. With thousands of people coming down to Chennai every year in January to witness this spectacle, Shaastra's reach is now beyond city, state and country and has a stake on international stage. Marquee events like IITM Research Expo and Industrial Open House remain the pillars of interaction between the student fraternity, industry and academia.
