Shaastra Main Quiz

Shaastra plays host to one of the biggest inter-collegiate tech quizzes in South India, the Shaastra Main Quiz. Some of the best quizzing minds from all over the country go heads up against each other in what is a test of agile thinking and Scientific knowledge. With the reputed and historic quizzing culture of IIT Madras brought to the fore in the prelims and finals, it promises to give the participants an adrenaline rush through highly exciting and versatile formats!
