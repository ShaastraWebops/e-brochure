ABP

Coding challenges for C and Python are very common. So Shaastra introduces one for the Arduino, the most popular electronics prototyping platform. This event is sure to have the quality blend of passionate coder and bright electrical engineers.


Eleckart

Eleckart of shaastra 2017 is a high profile show to compete on a big scale and showcase their talent where the participants play to build. Participants will be given a chance to ‘buy’ their required components based on virtual amount of cash they earned and then compete to build the best device based on the problem statement provided


SCDC (Shaastra Circuit Design Challenge)

Circuit Design happens to be major challenge to electrical engineers all over the world. SCDC, the flagship event of Electrical engineering, tests the ability of its participants to come up with a robust and compact circuit design for a socially relevant problem.


PCB Design

An event designed specifically for Electrical Engineering students. PCB's (Printed Circuit Boards) now form an integral part of any electronics system that you can find in your surroundings. Shaastra 2017 brings this opportunity to learn the skill of designing a circuit using the required tools, so that one day you can make your own robots dance to your tunes!
