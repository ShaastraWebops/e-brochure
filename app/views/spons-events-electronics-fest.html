Arduino Programmer's Challenge

This event is a coding challenge for Arduino programmers, an open source prototyping platform that is widely used by engineers, coders and researchers. Participants include passionate electrical engineers, electronics enthusiasts, and programmers.


Eleckart

ElecKart is a 3-round circuit design competition. After completing round 1, points earned from round 2 - a quiz - are used as currency to purchase components needed in the 3rd round. In round 3, the final round, participants are required to build a circuit specified by the Problem Statement using only the components they purchased. ElecKart was sponsored by Rohm in 2016.


Shaastra Circuit Design Challenge

SCDC is the flagship event of the Department of Electrical Engineering. Participants are tested on their ability to build a robust and compact circuit design for a real-world socially relevant problem.
