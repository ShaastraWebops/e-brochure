Arduino Based Programming

Are you interested in working on Arduino based electronic projects? Here is a unique opportunity to showcase your talent at the Arduino Programmers Challenge, Shaastra 2017. Team up for a brainstorming hand-on project opportunity. Shaastra awaits the great day when Arduino enthusiasts come, code and create.


Eleckart

Eleckart is combination of economics and electronics. From the first round of Eleckart, eight teams will be selected to compete in the finals. In the second round, the finalist teams will compete in a quiz, where the points earned will be converted into virtual money which they can use to buy the electronic components required to solve the problem statement in the final.


SCDC (Shaastra Circuit Design Challenge)

SCDC is the flagship event of the Department of Electrical Engineering. Participants are tested on their ability to build a robust and compact circuit design for a real-world socially relevant problem.


PCB Design

PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards) now form an integral part of any electronics system that you can find around you. Shaastra 2017 presents the opportunity to learn the skill of effective circuit board design, so that one day you can make your own devices!


Black Box

Black Box tests contestants' ability to reverse engineer circuits. The event also includes a round which involves circuit debugging.
