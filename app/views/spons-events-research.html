Research Paper Presentation

IIT Madras is the hotbed of innovation and in an effort to nurture this further, Shaastra 2016 opens its door for all researchers to ignite their imagery and present their novel work with the potential to change the world by solving its real life problems. This amalgamation of the brightest researchers at one place is the perfect place to explore all the recent developments in technology.


Industry Defined Problem

One of the most sought after events where the best minds at Shaastra battle it out to delve deep to ideate, innovate, conceptualise and provide feasible solutions with prototype or a detailed plan to the myriads of problems faced in the industry. Previous associations included GE, Shell, Cerner, Eaton etc. The diverse and multidisciplinary problems warrants sheer academic brilliance in all aspects of engineering with emphasis on creativity and practicality. This is one event proves to be the mother of all battles in providing true industrial solutions to the most challenging problems.
