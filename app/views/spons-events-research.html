Research Expo

IIT Madras is India’s best engineering college and one of its leading research institutions. A celebration of the advances in technology and research, visitors to this expo can interact with student researchers and learn of their work in cutting edge technology, and understand developments in engineering and science.The Expo in 2016 selected and hosted 35 teams of researchers and their exhibits. The Expo saw 1000+ visitors on the first day alone.


Vaayu Shakti

In this competition, students design and build a wind turbine which can generate enough electricity to power a small Indian coastal home. Devices are tested and judged at an IIT Madras laboratory which simulates coastal climatic conditions.



Indian Railways Design Challenge

The Railways Challenge is a another such unique opportunity for students to help solve one of India’s overlooked social issues. At Shaastra 2016, students designed solutions to help the elderly and disabled to board and disembark Indian trains. The challenge was submitted by The Indian Railways and was covered by The New Indian Express.
