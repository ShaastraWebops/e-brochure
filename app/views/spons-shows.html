Envisage

A novel techno cultural show specializing in designing your dreams in the field of science and technology, the only student organized event of its kind in the whole country. From basic elements such as LEDs to the futuristic devices such as Kinect, Envisage showcases unique way to blend science and entertainment.


Light and Sand Art

Shaastra 2016 witnessed India's best sand artist and India's only light painter Vivek patil who rose to fame by performances in National Television shows "Entertainment Ke Liye Kuchh Bhi Karega" and "India's Got Talent". His display of immense talent and artistic flamboyance have awed millions in the world, and people at Shaastra, who caught him live.


Airshow

​One of the biggest RC flying shows in the country, with planes and helicopters all twisting and twirling and fliers using the sky as the canvas for their masterpieces. Previous editions of Shaastra have witnessed some of world's best aero modellers like Wolfgang Krahofer, Gernot Bruckmann from Austria and 14 years old Jase Dussia from USA. Shaastra Air Show never failed in leaving its spectators in wonder!


Laser Show

The laser show, comprising a Graphic beam show and laser man act promised to be best in India. The artists at Shaastra 2015, mesmerized 3000+ audience with various different forms of laser provided an experience of a lifetime.


LED and Neon Show

​Shaastra 2016 brought international flavour with Ukraine’s finest entertainers Adelaida bringing one of the world’s best LED and NEON shows. Having performed 1000+ shows across the globe, across the globe at the world's most lavish locations, their zealous show raised energy levels over 9000!!


Extreme Sports Show

Shaastra 2016 had another international addition with world renowned stunt biker Yohan Triboulat. Hailing from France he has made his name by performing in stunt shows all around the globe for over 10 years. Jaws dropped as Yohan and his bike swing and jump at our custom made set.
