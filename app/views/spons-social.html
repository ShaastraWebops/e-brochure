Social Cause

Shaastra, over the years has evolved from being just a technical festival to an organization that augments and facilitates socially impactful initiatives. Handling issues ranging from those of the environment to the ones in the spheres of education and literacy, Shaastra always has striven to do it's bit to give back to the society.


Shaastra Computer Literacy for All aimed to strengthen computer infrastructure of institutions that serve the needy, using donated computer peripherals, not only helped the underprivileged, but also effected a reduction in the amount of e-waste generated.


'Pledge-A-Book' a campaign initiated by Shaastra aimed at making a difference in the fields of education and literacy, by promoting reading and encouraging development among underprivileged students. The campaign aims to collect books which are donated by patrons of Shaastra and use them for the betterment of the underprivileged, by setting up or supporting reading facilities at various schools, NGOs, orphanages, teaching centers.


The Pledge-A-Book campaign is our humble attempt at bringing change and helping design the dreams of the young. It is a platform where everyone can contribute and create an impact. We would appreciate your support in this initiative.
