Sudoku

A test of one’s logical thinking ability, it includes both the classical and variant Sudoku. From school kids to tech geeks, the event saw participants turning up in huge numbers.


Shaastra Cube Open

Affiliated to the WCA (World Cube Association), it is one of the largest international speedcubing competition in India. It witnesses global participation with young kids cracking complex problems in a matter of seconds. A highlight event of Shaastra, national records are smashed every year, including a successful attempt to break a Guinness Book record. The event serves as a platform for interaction between world class cubers and cubing enthusiasts.


Puzzle Champ

A test of analytical, tactical and logical abilities with no technical prerequisite, it promises to be one of the biggest participated event at Shaastra. With three days of thought provoking and mind boggling puzzles, the event is a huge crowd puller with extensive branding opportunities.


Rapid Chess

Affiliated to the All India Chess Federation, Shaastra will be introducing this event for the first time in 2017. It is expected to serve as a platform for budding Chess players to interact with the Grandmasters.


How Things Work

A wrong concept misleads the understanding. It is this understanding of the basic concepts that is tested in HTW. Garnering interest from avid quizzers to tech enthusiasts equipped with high school Science knowledge, a strong gut feeling and the ability to think on feet, this has been one of Shaastra’s mainstay events since the past decade. Promoting the spirit of scientific enquiry minus the boring equations or bland academic rigour, HTW is arguably the most unique quiz in the country.


Shaastra Treasure Hunt

We all get our neurons working and heart-beat rushing when it comes to Treasure hunts. Shaastra presents its own treasure hunt crafted beautifully to excite the tech-enthusiasts and interest the non-geeky ones too.
