Big Data Challenge

The event is based on Big Data Analytics. It comprises of a preliminary round ahead of Shaastra, followed by the final event during Shaastra on IITM campus


Case Study Challenge

Caselets containing a particular case, problem or a business situation will be handed to the participating teams who attempt to devise a solution for each problem. It comprises of a preliminary round followed by the finals conducted during Shaastra.


Consigliere

Management consulting event comprising of preliminary rounds before Shaastra followed by the finals during Shaastra. The event is based on case studies as well.


Start Up Wars

Virtual reality based event, in which each team of contestants are presented with a simulation of a startup situation for a particular product. The team which makes the highest profit at the end of a specified number of business cycles wins the event. Has the same preliminary round-finals format.


Traders Street

An entirely online event in which individuals compete in a virtual stock market competing to be the one with the best portfolio at the end of a specified time period. Event takes place prior to Shaastra.
