Mixels

Mixels is in the field of Image Processing where basic concepts are tested in the prelims, while the finals include advanced techniques like Augmented Reality and Detections.Sponsored by Samsung in 2016, 30 teams competed in the prelims. 14 qualified for the finals, and 3 teams were declared winners.

Triathlon

Triathlon is one event that tests three skills - Algorithms, logical reasoning, and web design. In 2016, Triathlon was sponsored by Visa, and saw 55 teams compete in the prelims. Of the 17 teams that went on to the final round, 3 were declared winners.


Online Programming Contest

The Shaastra OPC, a 3 hour long programming competition, has traditionally seen massive participation from India and abroad. Sponsored by Cognizant and conducted on CodeChef in 2016, two OPCs were conducted, which saw a total of 800 participants. The winners were from Poland and Peru.
