Robowars

The ultimate embodiment of the combination of robotics, aggression, creativity and fun! Robowars is a battle of high aggression, technique, control and damage ability. Two robots are put in an arena where they will engage in high intensity combat. Some weapons that past Robowars contestants have used - pneumatic spikes, flipping tusks, angle grinders, hurling maces and hydraulic pincers. Robowars is one event no one wants to miss!


Manual Robotics

Shaastra Manual Robotics is widely known to be one of the best in its kind in any college fest in South India. Unique problem statements with a perfect blend of complexity and thrill which puts to test design, creativity and technical skills promises to bring the absolute best out of participants! The variety in problem statements and the exposure that Shaastra Robotics offers is unparalleled.


Junkyard Wars

With just a junkyard of scraps of discarded material, contestants have to build functioning machines that can perform a specified task. Innovative design, technical knowledge, creativity and good engineering skills are essential to compete in this event.

Fire N Ice

Another high intensity remote controlled car race, Fire N Ice attracts RC enthusiasts from near and far. The smell of gasoline, the deafening sound of whirring engines, and intense competition is guaranteed in this long standing event at Shaastra.

Humanoid

"If you like building humanoid robots, this is a must-visit as Shaastra. There are two different competition - one for school students and the other for college students. School students build humanoids using mindstorms sets. College students build biped humanoids using micro-servos and microcontrollers like Arduino. Good programming skills, technical proficiency in electronics and robotics are necessary to compete in this event."

Microthon

Microthon is the place to show your skills, efficiency and optimisation ability. From crossing different paths and various obstacles to reducing the size as much as you can, while still being fast enough to conquer the arena.

Robo Ocena

RoboOceana is an underwater robotics event which has a unique problem statement in which each robot has to perform a variety of tasks under water.

Desmod

The challenge is to design a product keeping in mind its form and function, aesthetics and ergonomics with no compromise on its user friendliness.

Hand gesture controlled robots

Gesture-controlled robots are robots that are operated using simple gestures. The challenge is to build a bot that will follow your hand.
