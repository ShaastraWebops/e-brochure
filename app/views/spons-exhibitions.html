Tech and Innovation Fair

Shaastra hosts this fair every year aiming at promoting innovation in the world. The platform hosts the projects and exhibitions with novel ideas developed by the students across the globe. The developers get to exhibit their distinctive products to the eminent scientists, brilliant academicians and interact with the best brains of India.


Chess Automated

A unique opportunity to experience the game of chess without ever touching the real masterpieces. Chess Automated is a completely automated chess board which enables the user to play chess against a computer, against a computer and even against a human opponent present at any place in the world via the internet on a physical board.


Vintage Cars Exhibition

​​Shaastra 2016 unveiled the spectacular technological advancements in the automobile history with its very own Vintage Car Exhibition in collaboration with the Madras Heritage Motoring Club. Presented automobiles such as, Austin 8, 1952 Topolino, Morris 1000, Morris 8, which have been the marvels of technology bringing about a revolution in the automobile industry.


Arjun Tank

The Arjun Tank is a third generation main battle tank developed by India’s Defense Research and Development Orgnanization(DRDO), for the Indian Army. A Master Piece in the field of defense technology, this was a major attraction of Shaastra.
